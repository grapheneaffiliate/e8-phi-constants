## Overview

A theory that cannot be falsified is not science. The GSM makes specific, quantitative, zero-parameter predictions that current and near-future experiments can confirm or refute. This document compiles all falsifiable predictions in one place.

## Tier 1: Decisive Tests (Will Confirm or Falsify GSM)

### 1. CHSH Bell Bound

```
PREDICTION:  S_max = 4 − φ = 2.381966...
STANDARD QM: S_max = 2√2  = 2.828427...
```

**Falsification criterion:** A loophole-free measurement of S > 2.5 at ≥3σ significance.

**Current status:** No loophole-free Bell test has exceeded S = 2.5. Delft 2015: S = 2.38 ± 0.14.

**Required experiment:** Precision Bell test with σ(S) < 0.05, all loopholes closed. Achievable with current technology (superconducting nanowire detectors >95% efficiency, >10⁶ events).

**Timeline:** 2026–2028

### 2. Cosmic Birefringence

```
PREDICTION:  β₀ = arcsin(φ⁻³) = 0.2918...°
OBSERVATION: β = 0.30° ± 0.11° (Planck + WMAP)
```

**Falsification criterion:** β₀ deviates from 0.292° by >3σ when measured to ±0.01°.

**Additional predictions:**
- Redshift dependence: β(z) = β₀ × log_φ(1+z) / log_φ(1+z_CMB)
- 5-fold anisotropy pattern at ℓ = 5, 10, 15
- Non-zero β-galaxy cross-correlation: C_ℓ^{βg} ≠ 0 (unique to GSM)

**Falsification of sub-predictions:**
- β shows frequency dependence → favors axion models over GSM
- No 5-fold anisotropy at ℓ = 5 → geometric mechanism wrong
- No β-galaxy cross-correlation → GSM coupling mechanism wrong

**Timeline:** CMB-S4 (2030+), LiteBIRD (2028+)

### 3. Gravitational Wave Echoes

```
PREDICTION:  Δt_k = φ^{k+1} × 2GM/c³  (delay times in golden ratio)
             A_k  = φ⁻ᵏ                  (amplitude damping)
             θ_k  = k × 72° + 36°/φᵏ    (polarization rotation)
```

**Falsification criteria:**
- Delay ratios deviate from φ by >5%
- Amplitude ratios deviate from φ⁻¹ by >10%
- No 72° polarization signature
- No echoes detected with O5 sensitivity

**Unique signature:** The 72° = 360°/5 polarization rotation is exclusive to GSM. No other echo model predicts systematic polarization rotation.

**Timeline:** LIGO O5 (2027–2029), definitive with O6+ (2030+)

## Tier 2: Strong Constraints

### 4. Neutrino Mass Ordering

```
PREDICTION:  Normal Ordering (NO)
             δ_CP = π + arcsin(φ⁻³) = 193.65°
             Σm_ν = m_e × φ⁻³⁴(1 + εφ³) = 59.24 meV
```

**Falsification:** If Inverted Ordering is confirmed (δ_CP ≈ 270°), GSM is falsified.

**Current status:** δ_CP (NO) = 192° ± 20° — GSM within 0.08σ. JUNO will determine mass ordering by ~2028.

### 5. Born Rule Corrections

```
PREDICTION:  P(x) = |ψ(x)|² × [1 + O(φ⁻⁸)]
             φ⁻⁸ ≈ 0.0213
```

**Meaning:** The Born rule receives ~2% corrections from the discrete lattice structure. These are tiny but in principle measurable in extremely high-precision quantum experiments.

**Falsification:** Born rule corrections measured at a scale inconsistent with φ⁻⁸.

### 6. Proton Decay Timescale

```
PREDICTION:  M_X = M_Planck × φ⁻⁵ ≈ 1.1 × 10¹⁸ GeV
             τ_p ≈ 10³⁵ – 10³⁶ years
```

**Current limit:** τ_p > 10³⁴ years (Super-Kamiokande)

**Falsification:** Proton decay observed at rate inconsistent with φ⁻⁵ GUT scale. Or proton stability confirmed to >10³⁷ years.

**Timeline:** Hyper-Kamiokande (sensitivity ~10³⁵ years), DUNE

### 7. No Loophole-Free Bell Test Exceeding S = 2.5

```
PREDICTION:  For ALL future loophole-free Bell tests: S ≤ 2.382
```

This is a universal prediction — not for one experiment, but for all experiments ever. A single confirmed violation ends the framework.

## Tier 3: Speculative but Testable

### 8. φ-Spiral Casimir Enhancement

```
PREDICTION:  A Casimir cavity with φ-spiral geometry should show ~10,000×
             stronger vacuum energy extraction than flat parallel plates
```

**Basis:** The Casimir force F/A = π²ℏc/(240d⁴) has 240 in the denominator. E₈ has 240 root vectors. A φ-spiral cavity resonantly couples to the E₈ lattice structure, enhancing vacuum energy extraction.

**φ-harmonic coupling series:** κₙ = φ⁻ⁿ, converging to φ

**Status:** Speculative prediction awaiting experimental test. Requires precision Casimir force measurement in engineered geometries.

### 9. Dark Energy Equation of State

```
PREDICTION:  w = −1 (exact, from boundary crystallization)
```

**Falsification:** Any measurement of w ≠ −1 at >3σ.

**Timeline:** Euclid, DESI, Roman Space Telescope (ongoing)

### 10. Hubble Constant Resolution

```
PREDICTION:  H₀ = 100φ⁻¹(1 + φ⁻⁴ − 1/(30φ²)) = 70.03 km/s/Mpc
```

**Significance:** This sits between the CMB value (67.4) and the distance-ladder value (73.0), potentially resolving the Hubble tension through geometric effects at different scales.

## What Would Falsify GSM

Any **one** of these would be sufficient:

1. **S > 2.5** in a loophole-free Bell test at ≥3σ
2. **Inverted neutrino mass ordering** confirmed
3. **β₀ ≠ 0.292°** at >3σ with precision <0.01°
4. **GW echo delays not in golden ratio** at >5% precision
5. **w ≠ −1** at >3σ

## What Would Confirm GSM

Confirmation requires **multiple** independent tests:

1. **S_max = 2.38 ± 0.02** in precision Bell test — decisive
2. **β₀ = 0.292° ± 0.01°** from CMB-S4 — decisive
3. **φ-ratio GW echo delays** with 72° polarization rotation — decisive
4. **Normal ordering + δ_CP = 194° ± 5°** from DUNE — strong
5. **Σm_ν = 59 ± 5 meV** from cosmological surveys — strong

**The GSM puts itself at risk of falsification. The tests are underway.**
